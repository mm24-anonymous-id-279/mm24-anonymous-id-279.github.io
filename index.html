C. Comparison with Edge Refinement Methods

In this Section, refined segmentation masks are used to achieve seamless color fusion. To obtain the refined masks, we use the official repositories of two methods: SegRefiner and Video Mask Transfiner (VMT). We have listed the detailed runtime parameters below.

C1. DC (SegRefiner) vs MovingColor

Parameters:
tools/test.py configs/segrefiner/segrefiner_dis.py checkpoints/segrefiner_hr_latest.pth
MovingColor

C2. DC (VMT) vs MovingColor

Parameters:
-m tools.inference_with_vis  --masks --backbone resnet50 --model_path ./pretrained_model/checkpoint_r50_final.pth
MovingColor

D. Comparison with RSFNet

We utilize the official RSFNet code for video processing, employing the RSFNet-map trained on MIT-Adobe FiveK. The videos are segmented into clips, ensuring filter arguments for subsequent frames align with those of the initial frame within each clip.
